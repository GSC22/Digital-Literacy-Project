# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ General Safety Tips
- 🔐 Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- 🔑 Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- 🔍 Always verify links before clicking on them  
- 📵 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities  
- 🧠 Do not trust unknown calls, emails, or messages asking for personal information  
- 📰 Verify information before sharing to prevent spreading fake news  
- 🔒 Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches  
- 👀 Be cautious while downloading apps or files from unknown sources  

---

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- ❌ Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
- 📲 Do not approve unknown or suspicious payment requests  
- 🏦 Always double-check the receiver’s details before making payments  
- ⚠️ Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money  

---

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- 📞 Helpline Number: 1930 (24x7)  

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
Following these simple precautions can help students stay safe online and protect themselves from cyber threats in their daily digital activities.
