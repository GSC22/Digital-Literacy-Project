One situation where poor digital communication caused a serious problem is when a student sent an email to a professor using informal language, 
no subject line, and unclear content. As a result, the professor misunderstood the message and ignored the request, 
leading to the student missing an important deadline. This situation highlights how improper communication can create confusion and negatively 
impact academic performance.

This issue could have been avoided by following proper email etiquette, such as using a clear subject line, 
maintaining a formal tone, and writing a well-structured message. Being polite and precise in communication ensures that the message is understood correctly 
and taken seriously. This example shows the importance of responsible digital communication in academic and professional settings.
