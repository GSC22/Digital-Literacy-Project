# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments  
- Share useful, informative, and positive content  
- Protect your privacy by adjusting security settings  
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news  
- Use social media to build your personal and professional network  

---

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content  
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown sources  
- Do not misuse social media during academic or professional work  

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
Responsible use of social media helps students maintain a positive digital identity and ensures safe and effective online communication.
